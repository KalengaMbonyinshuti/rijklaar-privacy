RijKlaar Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

RijKlaar (“we”, “us”) is an app that helps you prepare for the Dutch CBR car theory exam. We collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what we store, why we store it, where it lives, and the rights you have over it.

1. Who is responsible

RijKlaar is operated by the RijKlaar app developer.
Contact for all privacy matters: braventaeurope@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

DataWhenDetails
Account dataOnly if you sign inYour email address, display name, and profile photo, as provided by the sign-in method you choose (Sign in with Apple, Google, or email/password).
Learning progressWhile you use the appWhich questions you have answered, points, day streak, per-topic scores, mock-exam results, and your chosen app language.
Anonymous usage statisticsWhile you use the appWhich questions are answered correctly/incorrectly, exam scores, and study-section completions — stored with no account id, name, or device identifier (the database schema does not even permit one). These cannot be linked to you; they exist to find and fix bad questions.
Nothing else—RijKlaar contains no advertising, no tracking SDKs, and no analytics that identify you. We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for tracking.

If you use RijKlaar without an account, all learning progress stays only on your device and never reaches our servers.

3. Why we process this data (legal basis)

  • To provide the service you asked for (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): saving your learning progress and syncing it across your devices when you sign in.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making, and we never sell it.

4. Where your data is stored

Account and progress data are stored with Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore) in the European Union — the Cloud Firestore database runs in the eur3 multi-region (European data centres). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Google processes this data on our behalf under the Google Cloud / Firebase EU Data Processing Terms, which include the EU Standard Contractual Clauses. Your data is not transferred outside the EU/EEA for storage.

5. How long we keep your data

Until you delete it. There is no retention beyond your account’s lifetime:

  • Delete your account in the app (Profile → Delete my account) and your account and all cloud data are removed immediately and permanently.
  • Progress stored only on your device is removed when you delete the app or use Delete my account.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object, and port your data. In the app:

  • Access (Art. 15): Profile → My data shows everything we store about you.
  • Erasure (Art. 17): Profile → Delete my account removes everything, instantly and permanently.
  • Portability (Art. 20): Profile → My data → Copy as text exports your data as JSON.

For any other request, email braventaeurope@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch supervisory authority: Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens (autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl).

7. Children

RijKlaar is intended for people preparing for the Dutch driving theory exam, generally 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

Access to your cloud data is restricted by Firebase security rules so that only your own signed-in account can read or write your data. Authentication tokens are managed by the Firebase SDK and stored in the device’s secure storage (iOS Keychain).

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top and inform you in the app before the change takes effect.
