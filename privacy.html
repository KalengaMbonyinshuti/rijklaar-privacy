Privacy Policy — RijKlaar

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the RijKlaar app ("RijKlaar", "the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. RijKlaar is a study app that helps you prepare for the Dutch CBR car theory exam. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data controller: Kalenga Mbonyinshuti, Wamel, The Netherlands. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at mbonyinshutijeremie@gmail.com.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the app's features:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier provided by that sign-in service. This is used to create and secure your account.
  • Study progress: Your practice results, scores, and any questions you flag are stored so you can track your progress across sessions.
  • Technical information: Our backend provider (Google Firebase) may process technical data such as your IP address on its servers as a normal part of delivering a secure online service.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or precise location.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or marketing trackers.
  • We do not include any analytics or crash-reporting SDKs in the app.
  • We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • To save and display your study progress and flagged questions.
  • To provide AI-generated explanations for exam questions (see Section 4).
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app.

4. AI-generated explanations

To help you understand exam questions, the app can generate plain-language explanations using an AI service provided by Anthropic. When this feature is used, only the exam question and answer text is sent to Anthropic to produce the explanation. No personal data, account information, or progress data is sent to Anthropic.

5. Third-party services

We use the following service providers, who process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC) — provides authentication, database storage for your account and progress, and hosting. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple — if you choose Sign in with Apple, Apple processes your sign-in. See apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Anthropic — provides the AI model that generates question explanations (question text only). See anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

6. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the app's core features (your account, progress tracking, and explanations) that you request by using the app.
  • Legitimate interests: to keep the app secure and functioning properly.

7. Data retention

We keep your account and progress data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account or ask us to delete your data, we will remove your personal data from our systems, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten");
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format;
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority — in the Netherlands, the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens (autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at mbonyinshutijeremie@gmail.com.

9. Children's privacy

RijKlaar is intended for users preparing for the Dutch driving theory exam, who are generally 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Data security

We use industry-standard measures provided by our service providers (including encryption in transit and secure authentication) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International data transfers

Some of our service providers (Google, Apple, Anthropic) may process data on servers located outside the European Economic Area. Where this occurs, these providers rely on appropriate safeguards (such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect your data.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated within the app where appropriate.
