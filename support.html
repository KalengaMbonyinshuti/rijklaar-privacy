Frequently asked questions

How do I change the app language?
Open the app, go to your Profile, and pick from the available languages. Practice and exam questions switch to the selected language.

Does the app work offline?
Yes. Practice questions, study material and exams are bundled in the app and work without an internet connection. An internet connection is only needed for sign-in, syncing your progress, and optional AI explanations.

How do I delete my account and data?
In the app, open Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data. You can also email us and we will delete it for you.

Is my progress saved?
When you are signed in, your progress and flagged questions are saved to your account and restored when you sign in on another device.

How do I report a mistake in a question?
Use the flag icon on any question, or email us the question and what looks wrong. We review every report.
